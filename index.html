About Me

You will always find me with my camera in one hand and an iced coffee in the other. I love living in Florida where the trees meet the ocean and anywhere can be my studio that best fits anyone's personality.



Although I started photographing as a child on my film camera, I truly began this passion in high school. From the moment I first picked up a camera at only 15 years old I was hooked. Showing the world a range of all of the different people and personalities in this life through my eyes is what made me eager to continue picking up a camera. I offer a rangeof different types of portraits, from engagement shoots to family christmas cards, anything capturing the big and small moments in life that people cherish forever is what I'm most passionate about.
